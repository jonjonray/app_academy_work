Authentic Spanish Paella

  • Description
  • Ingredients
  • Directions

A nice authentic spanish paella packed with protein. This recipe is based off of the paella wikipedia article found here

  • Spanish or Arborio Rice
  • Saffron
  • Chicken Stock
  • Shrimp
  • Mussels
  • Onions
  • Parsley
  • Salt and Peppers

  1. Cook onions with light oil until tender
  2. Add rice and sauté until slightly translucent. Immediately add chicken stock and bring to a boil.
  3. Throw everything else in there and simmer for 2 hours
  4. Season to taste
